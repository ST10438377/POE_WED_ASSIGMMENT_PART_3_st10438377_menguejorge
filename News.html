The funds were raised across three events in September

The Live Life Foundation would like to thank the many people and businesses involved in fundraising for the Royal Children’s Hospital Neurology & Movement Disorder Program. On 25th September, the Live Life Foundation will present a cheque to the Royal Children’s Hospital Foundation for $220,000.

Live Life Foundation Hosts Community Outreach Event with Volunteers

Recently, volunteers from the Live Life Foundation gathered for a community outreach event focused on food preparation and distribution. At the event, foundation members, wearing shirts with the organization’s logo, prepared sandwiches and organized essential items to support the local community. This event is part of the foundation’s ongoing efforts to promote health and well-being among youth, encouraging volunteer participation in social causes. Live Life Foundation remains committed to building a healthier and more resilient society.
